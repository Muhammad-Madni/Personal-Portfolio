Project 2

Project Description:
"Barrow Boy" is an exciting C++ game project that offers a fun and immersive gaming experience. In this game, players take on the role of a young entrepreneur managing their own fruit stand. The objective is to build and expand the business, facing unique challenges and making strategic decisions along the way.
Key Features:
Entrepreneurial Adventure:
Players step into the shoes of a barrow boy, making real-time decisions to run their virtual fruit stand.
Dynamic Gameplay:
Engage with diverse customers, manage inventory, and set prices to maximize profits and customer satisfaction.
Challenging Levels:
Progress through different game levels, each presenting new obstacles and opportunities.
Leaderboard:
Compete with players worldwide and aim to be the top barrow boy in the global market.
Project Impact:
"Barrow Boy" showcases the creative and technical skills of the developer, providing a delightful and engaging gaming experience. It demonstrates the potential of C++ in game development and serves as an example of combining entrepreneurship and programming in a fun and educational way.
